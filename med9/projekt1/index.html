Tiger

The tiger is the largest living cat species.

Location:

Tigers are usually found in rain forests, grasslands, savannas and even mangrove swamps.

Here is what tigers mainly eat:

  1. Sambar deer
  2. Wild pigs
  3. Water buffalo
  4. Antelope

More info:

The tiger was first described in 1758. Since the early 20th century, tiger populations have unfortunately lost at least 93% of their historic range. The tigers are an endangered species on the IUCN Red List. The global wild tiger population was estimated to be between 3000 - 4000 creatures, most of whom live in small isolated pockets. India hosts the largest current tiger population.
